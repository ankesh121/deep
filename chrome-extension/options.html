Usage guide

Step 1:Sign in to the DEEP

Step 2:Open the site you want to add as the lead

Step 3:Click on the DEEP: Add Lead extension icon ( on top right of the chrome window )

Step 4:Fill out the fields as the requirement, some fields like Title, Date of publication, URL will automatically get filled for most of the news sites or blogs

Step 5:Submit the lead, cheers!
